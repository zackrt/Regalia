What is Regalia ?
  • Regalia is a new cryptocurrency that essentially is a reward. Users that sign up and create an account get Regalia by logging their monthly rent payment
  • When you log your rent payment, you will receive equals amounts of Regalia in return
  • *You do not pay your rent with Regalia, you are rewarded Regalia each month. You must update your account monthly
  • Who is this app for? Ultimately, this app is for anyone that pays rent
Created by Zachary R. Toth M.P.A. to maximize social good.

Log In

  • Step 1: Register! Create new account with an email, secure password (hashed), full name and your monthly rent payment
  • Step 2: Click Login Tab! Use your email as your username, and type your password, and submit.
  • Step 3: Collect Regalia! Once you log in, you will receive your FREE Regalia cryptocurrency, you can hold or send another user your Regalia (coming soon)
  • Step 4: Update your personal account information (each month).
